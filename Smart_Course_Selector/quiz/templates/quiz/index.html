About Us

Choosing the right STEM module can be daunting for students due to the diverse
range of topics. Our Smart Module Selector is a user-friendly web app that
streamlines this decision-making process. By answering up to 15 curated questions,
students receive personalized module recommendations aligned with their interests,
skills, and career goals.


Using logical mapping and intelligent filtering, our tool narrows down options
based on factors like problem-solving preferences and mathematical inclination.
Whether interested in AI, Cybersecurity, Software Development, or Data Science,
our platform provides valuable insights to guide students in their academic journey
with confidence.
